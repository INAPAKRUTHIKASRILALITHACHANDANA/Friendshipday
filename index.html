Friendship Day is a special day to celebrate and appreciate our friends. It's a time to show gratitude for their support, companionship, and the joy they bring into our lives. People often exchange gifts, cards, or spend quality time together to express their love and appreciation for their friends. The day is all about recognizing the important role friends play in making our lives happier and more meaningful.

"Friendship isn't about who you've known the longest. It's about who walked into your life, said 'I'm here for you,' and proved it."
